About

Spaceland is a place where everyone can escape reality and enjoy the mysterious, yet calming nature of outer space. Guests can take a virtual tour of the stars, participate in a zero gravity simulation, and watch photos of space -- all from the comfort of their home. Spaceland was built in October 2020 - a time in which the public needed virtual escape opportunities.

Mission

Our mission is simple: embrace the unknown. Space can be scary for some people, yet it's vast expansiveness can provide ample opportunities to explore life.
